About Me

I am a PhD student at Hunan University. My research focuses on UAV swarm planning and control, especially robust multi-drone coordination in complex environments.

I received my BSc in Automation from Hunan University in 2024 and continued my doctoral study at the National Engineering Research Center for Robot Vision Perception and Control Technology.

Research Interests

  • UAV Swarm Planning

    Topology-aware trajectory planning for drone swarms under real constraints.

  • Multi-Agent Coordination

    Communication-delay robust collaboration mechanisms for distributed agents.

  • Real-World Deployment

    Bringing algorithms to real unmanned systems for stable and reliable operation.
